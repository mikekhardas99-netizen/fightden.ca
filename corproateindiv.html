    Traveling on the streets these days can be a scary trip for many if they don’t have basic self defense knowledge. Youth, women and those with mobility issues are in particular need to know how to spot potential dangerous encounters that could turn violent. Sensei Mike will take participants though the basics of top notch self defense techniques used by police and military personnel, and modern day mma fighters in the UFC.

    Did you know that 90% of altercations can be defended against with 5 basic techniques that you can learn in less than an hour?

    You will learn:  How to defend yourself from surprise attacks from behind or grabs from behind, hair and wrist grabbing and other grappling holds along with basic striking defense and offends like elbows and knees. Along with our world famous knife and gun disarms, which can be done by those with no previous experience at all!
